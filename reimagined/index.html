Reimagined Shaders

Reimagining the original Minecraft design.

Watch the Trailer

Complementary Shaders Reimagined is a Minecraft Java Edition shader pack that follows the standards of Complementary Shaders Version 4 while adding a new standard to the table. Embracing the blocky Minecraft style unlike Version 4. Reimagined is a challenge to achieve the great shader visuals without discarding the design decisions of Minecraft. But this does not mean the amount of complex effects are compromised. Reimagined has more effects going on by default than most popular shaderpacks, while still being good at performance. Trying to complement Minecraft in the best way possible, Reimagined aims to please everyone that love Minecraft.
