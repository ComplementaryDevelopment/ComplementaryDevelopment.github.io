Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2023 (v2)

This privacy policy explains how your personal information is processed when using our products like the Complementary Development website, Complementary shader packs, and the Complementary Installer. As a commitment to transparency and continuous improvement, we may periodically update this Privacy Policy for legal compliance, to refine its wording, add information regarding new products, and for similar purposes.

User Information Gathering

It's important to clarify that we do not directly collect any user information from our products. But we acknowledge that certain third-party services that our products use may gather anonymous user data for valid reasons. These third-party services are developed independently from us, and they can not be fully covered by our privacy policy. We will help you understand what services we are using, how/why are we using them, and point you to their privacy policies for you to be able to further evaluate how they handle your information.

Third-Party Services

The following services that we use with our products may collect user information:
- Google Services: The Complementarty Development website utilizes Google services, such as Google Analytics and Google Ads. Google may collect certain non-personal information for reasons like analyzing website traffic and evaluating performance. We opt for Google services due to their suitability in displaying website ads and the genuine enhancement they offer to the user experience. To gain comprehensive insight about Google's treatment of your information, we encourage you to review their privacy policy.
- Github Services: The Complementary Installer uses Github to host important files, and Github collects anonymous information about downloads. For more information about how Github handles your download data, we recommend referencing their privacy policy.
- External Links: Our products may contain links to external websites. We emphasize that we disclaim any responsibility for the privacy practices of these external websites. We advise reviewing their respective privacy policies prior to providing any personal information on such sites.
